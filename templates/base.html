How to use the Home Price Predictor

  1. Enter the number of bedrooms in your home
  2. Enter the number of bathrooms in your home
  3. Enter the condition of your home ( 1 = Needs Work, 3 = Fair, 5 = Like New)
  4. Enter the approximate Sq Ft of your home
  5. Press Predict
